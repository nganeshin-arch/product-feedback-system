## For Regular Users

### Browsing Products

#### View All Products

**Web:**
- Products are displayed on the home page
- Scroll through the product grid
- Each card shows:
  - Product image
  - Product name
  - Brief description
  - Category
  - Average rating (stars)
  - Number of reviews

**Mobile:**
- Products appear in a scrollable list
- Pull down to refresh
- Tap any product to see details

#### Search for Products

**Web:**
1. Use the search bar at the top
2. Type product name or keywords
3. Results filter automatically as you type
4. Clear search to see all products again

**Mobile:**
1. Tap the search bar
2. Enter search terms
3. Products filter in real-time
4. Tap X to clear search

#### Filter by Category

**Web:**
1. Click the category dropdown
2. Select a category:
   - Electronics
   - Home & Kitchen
   - Sports & Fitness
   - Books & Media
3. Select "All Categories" to reset

**Mobile:**
- Categories are shown on product cards
- Use search to find specific categories

### Viewing Product Details

**Web:**
1. Click on any product card
2. You'll see:
   - Full product information
   - Product images
   - Average rating and review count
   - All approved reviews
   - "Write a Review" button (if logged in)

**Mobile:**
1. Tap on any product
2. Scroll to see all details
3. Reviews are listed below product info
4. Tap "Write a Review" to submit feedback

### Submitting a Review

#### Step 1: Open Review Form

**Web:**
1. Navigate to a product detail page
2. Click **"Write a Review"** button
3. Review form appears

**Mobile:**
1. Open product details
2. Tap **"Write a Review"**
3. Review screen opens

#### Step 2: Rate the Product

- Click/tap on stars to select rating
- 1 star = Poor
- 2 stars = Fair
- 3 stars = Good
- 4 stars = Very Good
- 5 stars = Excellent

#### Step 3: Write Your Review

- Enter your thoughts about the product
- **Minimum:** 10 characters
- **Maximum:** 1000 characters
- Character counter shows remaining space
- Be honest and helpful!

#### Step 4: Submit

1. Click/tap **"Submit Review"**
2. Your review is sent for moderation
3. You'll see a success message
4. Review status: **Pending**

## For Moderators

### Accessing Moderation Dashboard

**Web:**
1. Login with moderator account
2. Click **"Moderation"** in the header
3. Dashboard shows all pending reviews

**Mobile:**
1. Login with moderator account
2. Tap **"Moderation"** button on home screen
3. Pending reviews list appears

### Reviewing Submissions

#### View Pending Reviews

**Web:**
- All pending reviews are listed
- Each shows:
  - Product name
  - User name
  - Rating (stars)
  - Review text
  - Submission date
  - Action buttons

**Mobile:**
- Scroll through pending reviews
- Tap any review to see full details
- Actions available at bottom

#### Approve a Review

**When to Approve:**
- Review is genuine and helpful
- Follows community guidelines
- No offensive content
- Relevant to the product

**How to Approve:**

**Web:**
1. Find the review
2. Click **"Approve"** button
3. Confirm action
4. Review becomes visible to all users
5. Product rating updates automatically

**Mobile:**
1. Tap on review
2. Tap **"Approve"** button
3. Confirm in dialog
4. Review is published

#### Reject a Review

**When to Reject:**
- Spam or advertising
- Offensive language
- Fake or misleading
- Off-topic content
- Violates guidelines

**How to Reject:**

**Web:**
1. Find the review
2. Click **"Reject"** button
3. Optionally enter rejection reason
4. Confirm action
5. Review is hidden from users

**Mobile:**
1. Tap on review
2. Tap **"Reject"** button
3. Enter reason (optional)
4. Confirm rejection

#### Remove an Approved Review

Sometimes you may need to remove a previously approved review.

**When to Remove:**
- Discovered to be fake
- Reported by users
- Violates guidelines
- Outdated or incorrect

**How to Remove:**

**Web:**
1. Go to product detail page
2. Find the approved review
3. Click **"Remove"** button (moderator only)
4. Confirm removal
5. Product rating recalculates

**Mobile:**
1. Navigate to product
2. Find the review
3. Tap **"Remove"**
4. Confirm action

### Moderation Best Practices

#### Be Fair and Consistent

- Apply same standards to all reviews

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is the app free to use?**
A: Yes, completely free for all users.

**Q: Do I need an account to browse products?**
A: No, but you need an account to submit reviews.

**Q: Can I use the same account on web and mobile?**
A: Yes! Your account works on both platforms.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Contact support for password reset assistance.

### Review Questions

**Q: How long does review approval take?**
A: Usually within 24-48 hours, depending on moderator availability.

**Q: Why was my review rejected?**
A: Check the rejection reason provided by the moderator. Common reasons include spam, offensive content, or guideline violations.

**Q: Can I edit my review after submission?**
A: No, reviews cannot be edited once submitted. You can submit a new review if the old one is removed.

**Q: Can I review the same product multiple times?**
A: No, only one review per product per user.

**Q: What if I change my mind about my rating?**
A: Contact a moderator to remove your review, then submit a new one.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why can't I see my review?**
A: Reviews must be approved by a moderator before appearing publicly.

**Q: The app says "No internet connection"**
A: Check your WiFi or mobile data. The app requires internet for most features.

**Q: Can I use the mobile app offline?**
A: You can view previously loaded products and reviews offline, but cannot submit new reviews or login.

**Q: Why is the app slow?**
A: Try:
- Closing and reopening the app
- Checking your internet connection
- Clearing app cache
- Updating to latest version

### Moderator Questions

**Q: How do I become a moderator?**
A: Contact the system administrator. Moderator accounts are created manually.

**Q: Can I approve my own reviews?**
A: No, this would be a conflict of interest.

**Q: What if I accidentally reject a good review?**
A: Contact an administrator to restore it.

**Q: How many moderators are there?**
A: This varies by system configuration.

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Writing Great Reviews

1. **Be Specific**
   - Mention specific features
   - Describe your use case
   - Give concrete examples

2. **Be Balanced**
   - Mention both pros and cons
   - Be fair and objective
   - Acknowledge different perspectives

3. **Be Helpful**
   - Think about what others want to know
   - Answer common questions
   - Provide context

4. **Be Clear**
   - Use proper grammar and spelling
   - Organize your thoughts
   - Keep it concise

5. **Be Honest**
   - Share your genuine experience
   - Don't exaggerate
   - Disclose any conflicts of interest

### For Effective Moderation

1. **Stay Objective**
   - Focus on guidelines, not opinions
   - Don't let personal bias influence decisions
   - Be consistent

2. **Be Timely**
   - Check pending reviews daily
   - Respond to reports quickly
   - Keep the queue moving

3. **Communicate Clearly**
   - Provide specific rejection reasons
   - Be professional and respectful
   - Help users improve

4. **Document Decisions**
   - Keep notes on difficult cases
   - Track patterns
   - Report issues

5. **Seek Help**
   - Consult other moderators
   - Escalate unclear cases
   - Ask for guidance when needed

### For Better Experience

1. **Keep App Updated**
   - Install updates when available
   - Check for new features
   - Read release notes

2. **Use Good Internet**
   - WiFi is faster than mobile data
   - Stable connection prevents errors
   - Download content when online

3. **Provide Feedback**
   - Report bugs
   - Suggest improvements
   - Share your experience

4. **Be Patient**
   - Reviews take time to moderate
   - App may be slow during peak times
   - Contact support if issues persist

5. **Follow Guidelines**
   - Read and understand rules
   - Respect other users
   - Help maintain quality

## Glossary

**Review:** A text-based opinion about a product with a star rating

**Rating:** A numerical score from 1-5 stars

**Moderation:** The process of reviewing and approving user submissions

**Moderator:** A user with special permissions to approve/reject reviews

**Pending:** A review waiting for moderator approval

**Approved:** A review that has been accepted and is visible to all

**Rejected:** A review that has been declined and is not visible

**Product:** An item or service that can receive reviews

**Category:** A classification grouping similar products
