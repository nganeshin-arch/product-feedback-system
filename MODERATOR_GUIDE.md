## Moderator Role

### Responsibilities

As a moderator, you are responsible for:

- **Quality Control:** Ensuring reviews meet community standards
- **Spam Prevention:** Identifying and rejecting spam or fake reviews
- **User Safety:** Removing offensive or harmful content
- **Fairness:** Applying guidelines consistently to all users
- **Timeliness:** Processing reviews in a reasonable timeframe
- **Communication:** Providing clear feedback when rejecting reviews

### Authority

Moderators can:
- ✅ View all pending reviews
- ✅ Approve reviews
- ✅ Reject reviews with reasons
- ✅ Remove approved reviews
- ✅ View moderation history
- ✅ Access moderation statistics

Moderators cannot:
- ❌ Edit user reviews
- ❌ Change user ratings
- ❌ Delete user accounts
- ❌ Modify product information
- ❌ Override other moderators' decisions without cause

### Code of Conduct

Moderators must:
- Be fair and impartial
- Follow community guidelines
- Respect user privacy
- Act professionally
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report serious violations

## Getting Started

### Accessing Moderation Tools

#### Web Application

1. Login with your moderator account
2. Look for **"Moderation"** link in the header
3. Click to access the moderation dashboard
4. You'll see all pending reviews

#### Mobile Application

1. Login with your moderator account
2. Tap **"Moderation"** button on home screen
3. Pending reviews list appears
4. Tap any review to see details

### First Time Setup

1. **Read Guidelines:** Familiarize yourself with review guidelines
2. **Practice:** Review a few submissions to get comfortable
3. **Ask Questions:** Contact senior moderators if unsure
4. **Start Slow:** Begin with clear-cut cases
5. **Build Confidence:** Gradually handle more complex reviews

## Review Guidelines

### Approval Criteria

Approve reviews that:

✅ **Are Genuine**
- Based on real experience
- Specific and detailed
- Consistent with product

✅ **Are Helpful**
- Provide useful information
- Help others make decisions
- Answer common questions

✅ **Follow Rules**
- Appropriate language
- Relevant to product
- Proper length (10-1000 chars)
- Valid rating (1-5 stars)

✅ **Are Well-Written**
- Clear and understandable
- Proper grammar (minor errors OK)
- Organized thoughts

### Rejection Criteria

Reject reviews that contain:

❌ **Spam**
- Advertising or promotions
- Irrelevant links
- Repeated content
- Bot-generated text

❌ **Offensive Content**
- Profanity or vulgar language
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Discriminatory remarks

❌ **Fake Reviews**
- Obviously fabricated
- Competitor sabotage
- Incentivized reviews
- Copied from elsewhere

❌ **Inappropriate Content**
- Off-topic
- Personal information
- Illegal activities
- Dangerous advice

❌ **Low Quality**
- Too short or vague
- Incomprehensible
- All caps or excessive punctuation
- No actual review content

### Gray Areas

Some reviews are not clearly good or bad. Consider:

**Negative Reviews:**
- Negative opinions are valid if genuine
- Don't reject just because rating is low
- Look for constructive criticism
- Verify complaints are reasonable

**Short Reviews:**
- "Great product!" might be too short
- "Works perfectly, fast shipping, highly recommend" is better
- Minimum 10 characters is enforced
- Look for substance, not just length

**Emotional Reviews:**
- Strong emotions are OK if appropriate
- Distinguish between passion and aggression
- Allow frustration if expressed respectfully
- Reject if crosses into offensive territory

**Borderline Language:**
- Minor grammar issues are acceptable
- Slang is OK if not offensive
- Non-native speakers may have errors
- Focus on intent, not perfection

## Moderation Actions

### Approving Reviews

#### When to Approve

Approve when review:
- Meets all approval criteria
- Provides value to readers
- Follows community guidelines
- Is genuine and helpful

#### How to Approve

**Web:**
1. Review the submission carefully
2. Verify it meets guidelines
3. Click **"Approve"** button
4. Confirm action
5. Review becomes visible immediately

**Mobile:**
1. Tap on the review
2. Read full content
3. Tap **"Approve"** button
4. Confirm in dialog
5. Review is published

#### After Approval

- Review appears on product page
- Product rating updates automatically
- User can see their approved review
- Action is logged in moderation history

### Rejecting Reviews

#### When to Reject

Reject when review:
- Violates guidelines
- Contains spam or offensive content
- Is fake or misleading
- Provides no value

#### How to Reject

**Web:**
1. Identify guideline violation
2. Click **"Reject"** button
3. Enter rejection reason (recommended)
4. Confirm action
5. Review is hidden from users

**Mobile:**
1. Tap on the review
2. Tap **"Reject"** button
3. Enter reason in dialog
4. Confirm rejection
5. Review is rejected

#### Rejection Reasons

Be specific and helpful:

**Good Reasons:**
- "Contains advertising/spam"
- "Offensive language used"
- "Review is off-topic"
- "Too short, lacks detail"
- "Appears to be fake"

**Poor Reasons:**
- "Bad"
- "No"
- "Rejected"
- (No reason given)

#### After Rejection

- Review is not visible to public
- User may see rejection reason
- User can submit new review
- Action is logged

### Removing Approved Reviews

Sometimes you need to remove a previously approved review.

#### When to Remove

Remove approved reviews if:
- Discovered to be fake after approval
- Reported by multiple users
- Contains hidden violations
- Circumstances changed
- Error in original approval

#### How to Remove

**Web:**
1. Navigate to product page
2. Find the approved review
3. Click **"Remove"** button (moderator only)
4. Enter removal reason
5. Confirm action

**Mobile:**
1. Go to product details
2. Locate the review
3. Tap **"Remove"**
4. Enter reason
5. Confirm removal

#### After Removal

- Review disappears from product page
- Product rating recalculates
- User is notified (if configured)
- Action is logged with reason

## Best Practices

### Consistency

**Apply Standards Uniformly:**
- Use same criteria for all reviews
- Don't favor certain users or products
- Be predictable in decisions
- Document difficult cases

**Create Personal Guidelines:**
- Note edge cases you encounter
- Develop consistent responses
- Share with other moderators
- Update as needed

### Efficiency

**Work Smart:**
- Process clear cases quickly
- Flag complex cases for review
- Use keyboard shortcuts (web)
- Batch similar reviews

**Prioritize:**
- Handle oldest reviews first
- Address reported reviews promptly
- Focus on high-traffic products
- Clear backlog regularly

### Quality

**Take Your Time:**
- Read reviews thoroughly
- Consider context
- Check user history if available
- Don't rush decisions

**When in Doubt:**
- Err on side of approval
- Consult other moderators
- Document reasoning
- Learn from feedback

### Communication

**Be Clear:**
- Provide specific rejection reasons
- Use professional language
- Be respectful and helpful
- Offer guidance for improvement

**Be Constructive:**
- Explain what was wrong
- Suggest how to improve
- Encourage resubmission
- Maintain positive tone

### Self-Care

**Avoid Burnout:**
- Take regular breaks
- Don't moderate for too long
- Share workload with team
- Step away if frustrated

**Stay Objective:**
- Don't take reviews personally
- Separate your opinions from guidelines
- Recognize when you're biased
- Ask for help when needed

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Negative but Valid Review

**Review:** "Product broke after 2 weeks. Poor quality. Would not recommend. 1 star."

**Decision:** ✅ APPROVE

**Reasoning:**
- Genuine experience
- Specific timeframe
- Relevant to product
- Helps others make informed decision
- Negative opinions are valid

### Scenario 2: Spam Review

**Review:** "Check out my website for great deals! www.spam-site.com"

**Decision:** ❌ REJECT

**Reasoning:**
- Clear advertising
- Not about the product
- Contains external link
- Spam content

**Rejection Reason:** "This review contains advertising and is not about the product."

### Scenario 3: Too Short

**Review:** "Good"

**Decision:** ❌ REJECT

**Reasoning:**
- Too vague
- Provides no useful information
- Doesn't help other users
- Below quality threshold

**Rejection Reason:** "Review is too short. Please provide more details about your experience with the product."

### Scenario 4: Offensive Language

**Review:** "This [profanity] product is [profanity] terrible!"

**Decision:** ❌ REJECT

**Reasoning:**
- Contains profanity
- Violates community guidelines
- Offensive to readers
- Can be rewritten appropriately

**Rejection Reason:** "Review contains offensive language. Please resubmit without profanity."

### Scenario 5: Competitor Sabotage

**Review:** "Terrible product. Buy [competitor name] instead. Much better quality."

**Decision:** ❌ REJECT

**Reasoning:**
- Promotes competitor
- May be fake
- Not helpful for this product
- Appears malicious

**Rejection Reason:** "Review appears to be promoting a competitor product rather than reviewing this item."

### Scenario 6: Emotional but Valid

**Review:** "I'm so disappointed! I had high hopes but the product didn't work as advertised. The battery life is terrible - only lasts 2 hours instead of the claimed 10. Save your money."

**Decision:** ✅ APPROVE

**Reasoning:**
- Genuine frustration
- Specific complaint (battery life)
- Factual claims
- Helpful warning to others
- No offensive language

### Scenario 7: Non-English Review

**Review:** "Très bon produit, je recommande!"

**Decision:** Depends on policy

**Options:**
- Reject if English-only policy
- Approve if multilingual allowed
- Use translation tool to verify content

### Scenario 8: Review of Shipping, Not Product

**Review:** "Shipping took forever and box was damaged. 1 star."

**Decision:** ❌ REJECT

**Reasoning:**
- About shipping, not product
- Not relevant to product quality
- Doesn't help evaluate product
- Should contact customer service

**Rejection Reason:** "This review is about shipping rather than the product itself. Please contact customer service for shipping issues."

### Scenario 9: Borderline Length

**Review:** "Works great, very happy with purchase!"

**Decision:** ✅ APPROVE (if meets 10 char minimum)

**Reasoning:**
- Meets minimum length
- Positive experience
- Clear opinion
- While brief, provides value

### Scenario 10: Suspected Fake Positive

**Review:** "AMAZING!!! BEST PRODUCT EVER!!! BUY NOW!!! 5 STARS!!!"

**Decision:** ❌ REJECT

**Reasoning:**
- Excessive enthusiasm
- All caps
- Excessive punctuation
- Lacks specific details
- Appears fake or incentivized

**Rejection Reason:** "Review appears inauthentic. Please provide specific details about your experience with the product."

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Issue:** Can't access moderation dashboard

**Solution:**
- Verify you're logged in with moderator account
- Check your role permissions
- Try logging out and back in
- Contact administrator if problem persists

**Issue:** Review won't approve/reject

**Solution:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try again in a few minutes
- Report technical issue if continues

**Issue:** Accidentally approved wrong review

**Solution:**
- Navigate to product page
- Find the review
- Use "Remove" action
- Enter reason for removal
- Contact administrator if needed

**Issue:** Unsure about a review

**Solution:**
- Take more time to evaluate
- Consult moderation guidelines
- Ask another moderator
- Document your reasoning
- Err on side of approval if truly unsure

**Issue:** User complaining about rejection

**Solution:**
- Review your decision
- Check if reason was clear
- Explain guidelines politely
- Offer to reconsider if appropriate
- Escalate if user is abusive
