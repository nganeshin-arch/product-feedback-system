# Product Feedback System - Android App Installation Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you install the Product Feedback System mobile app on your Android device.

## System Requirements

- Android device running **Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher**
- At least **50 MB** of free storage space
- Internet connection for app functionality

## Installation Steps

### Step 1: Enable Installation from Unknown Sources

Since this app is not from the Google Play Store, you need to allow installation from other sources:

#### For Android 8.0 - 12:
1. Open **Settings** on your device
2. Go to **Security** or **Privacy**
3. Find **Install unknown apps** or **Install from unknown sources**
4. Select your **browser** (Chrome, Firefox, etc.) or **File Manager**
5. Toggle **Allow from this source** to ON

#### For Android 13+:
1. When you try to install the APK, Android will prompt you
2. Tap **Settings** in the prompt
3. Toggle **Allow from this source** to ON
4. Go back and continue installation

### Step 2: Download the APK

1. On your Android device, open your web browser
2. Navigate to the download link provided (e.g., GitHub Releases page)
3. Tap the **Download APK** button
4. Wait for the download to complete
5. You'll see a notification when download finishes

**Download Link:** [Insert your download link here]

### Step 3: Install the App

1. Open your **Downloads** folder or tap the download notification
2. Tap on the **app-release.apk** file
3. You may see a warning - tap **Install anyway** or **Install**
4. Wait for installation to complete (usually 10-30 seconds)
5. Tap **Open** to launch the app, or **Done** to finish

### Step 4: First Launch

1. When you first open the app, you may be asked to grant permissions
2. Tap **Allow** for any requested permissions (internet access, storage)
3. You'll see the login screen

### Step 5: Create an Account or Login

#### New Users:
1. Tap **Sign Up**
2. Enter your name, email, and password
3. Tap **Sign Up** button
4. You're ready to use the app!

#### Existing Users:
1. Enter your email and password
2. Tap **Login**
3. You're in!

## Using the App

### Browse Products
- Scroll through the product list on the home screen
- Use the search bar to find specific products
- Tap any product to see details and reviews

### Submit a Review
1. Open a product detail page
2. Tap **Write a Review**
3. Select a star rating (1-5 stars)
4. Write your review (10-1000 characters)
5. Tap **Submit**
6. Your review will be pending moderator approval

### View Your Reviews
- Your submitted reviews will show as "Pending" until approved
- Once approved, they'll appear on the product page

### Moderator Features
If you're a moderator:
1. Tap **Moderation** button on home screen
2. See all pending reviews
3. Tap **Approve** or **Reject** for each review

## Troubleshooting

### "App not installed" Error

**Solution 1:** Uninstall any previous version
1. Go to Settings > Apps
2. Find "Product Feedback"
3. Tap **Uninstall**
4. Try installing again

**Solution 2:** Clear download cache
1. Go to Settings > Apps > Downloads
2. Tap **Clear Cache**
3. Download and install again

**Solution 3:** Restart your device
- Sometimes a simple restart fixes installation issues

### "Parse Error" Message

This usually means the APK file is corrupted:
1. Delete the downloaded APK
2. Download it again
3. Make sure download completes fully
4. Try installing again

### App Crashes on Launch

**Solution 1:** Clear app data
1. Go to Settings > Apps > Product Feedback
2. Tap **Storage**
3. Tap **Clear Data**
4. Open the app again

**Solution 2:** Reinstall the app
1. Uninstall the app
2. Restart your device
3. Install the app again

**Solution 3:** Check internet connection
- The app requires internet to function
- Make sure you're connected to WiFi or mobile data

### Can't Login

**Check your credentials:**
- Make sure email and password are correct
- Email is case-sensitive
- Password must be at least 6 characters

**Check internet connection:**
- The app needs internet to authenticate
- Try switching between WiFi and mobile data

**Server issues:**
- If the backend server is down, you won't be able to login
- Try again later or contact support

### Products Not Loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh the product list
3. Try closing and reopening the app
4. Clear app cache (Settings > Apps > Product Feedback > Clear Cache)

### Reviews Not Submitting

1. Make sure you're logged in
2. Check that your review is 10-1000 characters
3. Ensure you've selected a star rating
4. Check your internet connection
5. Try again in a few minutes

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this app safe?
Yes! The app is open source and you can review the code on GitHub.

### Why isn't it on the Play Store?
This is a demonstration/portfolio project. Play Store distribution requires a developer account and ongoing maintenance.

### Does it work offline?
Partially. You can view previously loaded products and reviews offline, but you need internet to:
- Login/signup
- Submit new reviews
- Load new products
- Sync data

### Can I use it on iOS?
Currently, only Android is supported. iOS version may be added in the future.

### How do I become a moderator?
Contact the system administrator. Moderator accounts are created manually for security reasons.

### Is my data secure?
Yes! All communication uses HTTPS encryption. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

### Can I delete my account?
Contact support to request account deletion. We'll remove all your data per GDPR requirements.
